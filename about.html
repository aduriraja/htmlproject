About Us

Last Updated : 5 January, 2025

10000coders is a tech education platform and a comprehensive learning ecosystem that focuses on building practical, job-ready skills in software development. Through immersive, hands-on training and personalized mentorship, it transforms beginners into industry-ready developers. Our 6-month immersive programs combine real-world projects, one-on-one mentorship, and industry-aligned curriculum — backed by a strong placement support system that stays with you until you get hired. We don't just train; we mentor, guide, and activate your career — giving you the tools, confidence, and support to secure your first role in tech.

Your Journey at 10000coders

At 10000coders, your learning path is driven by daily practice, weekly challenges, and consistent feedback. The journey begins with foundational sessions and evolves into building real-world projects that solve real user problems. At the heart of our placement process lies the CRPR checkpoint – an internal review of your Communication, Problem-Solving, Resume, and Projects to ensure you're fully ready for hiring.
Once CRPR is cleared, you enter our Placement Activation Pool. We start matching you with our hiring partners, schedule interviews, and assist with everything from mock interviews to follow-ups. Throughout the entire journey, a dedicated Program Manager provides 1-on-1 mentorship — tracking your progress, offering tailored feedback, and staying by your side until you get your job offer.

Why Choose 10000coders

We don't just teach code — we teach you how to build a career in tech. Our programs are built with a single goal: real job outcomes. That's why our learning model includes structured guidance, industry-relevant projects, and a full-stack of support — from coding help to placement services. What makes us different is our commitment to stand by our students until they get placed, ensuring every learner gets personalized attention and career acceleration.
At 10000coders, we believe consistent effort, proper mentorship, and the right opportunities can transform anyone into a high-value engineer or designer — regardless of background.
